2025



Problem Statement


How might we motivate the younger generation to stay active in order to maintain social and mental wellbeing ?

Value Proposition

Steps is the walk companion app that allows the productive age group to find purpose in walking by gamifying daily walks into scavenger hunts.


Key Features

1. Daily Scavenger Hunt Prompts

Give purpose to each walk, create fomo for people who does not have the app

2. Streaks count and points counter

Rewards, streaks, or challenges motivate consistency

3. Photo capture and geotagging

Users snap photos of what they find; the app automatically records where and when each picture was taken.

4. Activity Tracking

Built-in metrics like miles walked, steps taken, time spent, and mapped routes encourage fitness awareness and documentation

Competitors

1. Direct Competitors

Pokemon Go, Geocaching

2. Secondary Competitors

Strava

3. Indirect Competitors

Tiktok, Royal Match
