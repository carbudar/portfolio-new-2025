About Me

I am an aspiring a multidimensional creator with a passion for promoting interactions through pixels and circuits.

Hi again, I am Carla Budar, currently studying Design and Technology in Parsons School of Design. I am enthusiastic about using technology and creativity as tools for building fun, intuitive, and engaging experiences. Whether it's developing dynamic web experiences, experimenting with interactive technology, or modeling innovative products, I aim to maintain a streamlined design approach. I believe, good designs should be both fresh, exciting, yet user-friendly; striking the right balance between practicality and play.
